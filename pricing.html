Villa Sofia is a lovely holiday home of 240 sq. m. located in the idyllic settlement of Amoni. It is a new residence with a nice garden, table tennis, a full size billiard table and all necessary facilities for a relaxed and pleasant stay, ideal for families and couples who want to spend a relaxing holiday together. The location of the Villa is peaceful, with complete privacy and just a few minutes drive from wonderful beaches. These and the local tavern can be reached within 5-30 minutes walk.

Villa Sofia is a 1 hour and 20-minute drive from the city of Athens and a one and a half-hour drive from Athens International Airport. The house is situated on a 1-acre plot and features 4 bedrooms, 4 bathrooms, a fully equipped kitchen, a 45-inch flat-screen smart TV, a mini hi-fi music system, free Wi-Fi, and a spacious common room with a full-size pool table and ping pong. A barbecue set is available free of charge for all guests. The villa can accommodate 9 people, and there are 3 additional folding beds. The property has three levels. On the first floor, where the main entrance is located, there is a spacious living room with a fireplace, a fully equipped kitchen, a dining area, a bedroom with a double bed, a bathroom with a shower, and a furnished terrace with excellent sea views. The floor has direct access to the garden. On the second floor, there are three bedrooms: A master bedroom with a double bed, a private bathroom, and a balcony with beautiful sea views. A bedroom with a desk and a balcony with stunning views of the sea and mountains. A third bedroom with two single beds, a desk, and a balcony with sea and mountain views. There is also a second bathroom with a bathtub and shower, and three additional folding beds available for children. On the ground floor, which also has direct access to the garden, there is a large common room (playroom) with a full pool table, a ping pong table, two more single beds, a sofa, a work desk, two chests of drawers, a bathroom with a shower, a storage room with a washing machine, and a second refrigerator. The living area has five air conditioning units, one for each bedroom and one in the living room. The garden, spread over three levels, has many trees and aromatic plants with plenty of play space for children. Enjoying your morning coffee there or having dessert in the evening offers a wonderful and relaxing view of the sea and the bay below. The neighboring plot also has many trees and is available for guests to enjoy.

Guests have access to the entire 1-acre plot and the neighboring plot, including the multi-level garden, terraces, parking area, barbecue, as well as everything else within the property, which is exclusively yours to enjoy. There is free parking space for three cars outside the house, and there is also room for three more cars inside the property's garage. It is easy to carry luggage and bags in and out of the house, as all parking spots are within 8 meters of the front door and the kitchen door, with no stairs in the way.

Villa Sofia is the perfect place to truly escape. A romantic house built with love for families and friends to enjoy. The unique architecture, beautiful design, and decor, along with the stunning views from every angle made possible by the large windows and openings around, make this residence ideal for a pleasant and relaxing vacation. The pool table, ping pong, and other amenities provided make the villa enjoyable for both adults and children. Adults can relax or enjoy their meals on one level, while children can have fun playing in the playroom or in the garden. Apart from Mikro Amoni bay, you can also swim at Fragolimano bay, where a canteen is open during the summer, as well as at Megalo Amoni bay and Kalogerolimano bay. All are within 2-10 minutes by car (1-3 kilometers away). One of the best seafood taverns in the Peloponnese, according to Tripadvisor, is just 1 kilometer from the villa.
