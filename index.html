Location of Villa Sofia

Location Overview

Villa Sofia is located in Amoni, Corinthia, offering calm seaside living and easy access to Nafplio, Epidaurus, Ancient Corinth, and the Saronic islands.
Amoni, Corinthia, Sofiko, Ancient Corinth, Peloponnese, Nafplio, Argos, Epidaurus, Olympia, Mycenae, Mani. Corinthia, Pefkali, Peloponnese, Greece Villa Sofia is located in Amoni, Corinthia, Sofiko, Saronic, Pefkali, Soligia, Nafplio, Loutraki, Epidaurus, Peloponnese, Western Greece, and the Ionian. Amoni is a peaceful coastal community where guests can relax by the sea and enjoy natural surroundings. The area has multiple bays and dining options, including local seafood tavernas and traditional Greek fast-food spots. Recreational facilities near Mikro Amoni beach include tennis courts, basketball courts, and a playground. Nearby beaches such as Megalo Amoni, Fragkolimano, and Kalogerolimano are 3–10 minutes by car. Villa Sofia is also ideally located for excursions (30–90 minutes by car) to Epidaurus (43 km), Corinth Canal (25 km), Mycenae (69 km), Ancient Corinth (37 km), and Nafplio (64 km). Popular day-trip islands include Spetses, Poros, and Hydra; Porto Heli (80 km) offers regular ferry departures for these destinations.
Transportation & Accessibility
We recommend renting a car for maximum convenience. The nearest bus stop is 6 km away and the closest train station is 26 km away, while driving provides easy access to local supermarkets, tavernas, beaches, and key regional sights.
